Why Identity Is the New Perimeter

IAM / Entra ID • By Augustin

Identity Attacks Are Increasing

Attackers now target user identities more than firewalls. Password spraying, token theft, and MFA fatigue are common attack vectors.

Zero Trust Starts with Identity

Zero Trust assumes no user or device is trusted by default. Identity becomes the foundation of access decisions.

Key Identity Protections

  • MFA everywhere
  • Conditional Access policies
  • Privileged Identity Management (PIM)
  • Identity Protection risk policies
